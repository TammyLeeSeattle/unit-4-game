Fruit Salad... the Game.

This is a game where your choices make the difference between a successful fruit salad (yum!) or a bunch of broken fruit.

Instructions

To mysterious fruit salad is only achievable when you guess the target score required to make it. There are four fruits below. Each fruit hides a different amount of points that will help you reach your target score -- but there's a catch! You won't know the hidden points until you click on them :)

Keep clicking on the various fruit to figure the hidden points! Then when you've figured out the value of each fruit, keep clicking until you reach your target score.
